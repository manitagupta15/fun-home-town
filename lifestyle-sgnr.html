Lifestyle in Sri Ganganagar

Sri Ganganagar shares its border with Punjab. So a lot of influence of this state is seen in the lifestyle of the people here. Bagri and Punjabi are the main languages of the people, apart from Hindi and Rajasthani. Despite restoring the cultural values, the people have adapted many traits of modern lifestyle as well. The city offers various healthy lifestyle choices with availability of services by beauty salons, spas, boutiques, fitness centers, etc.
